Buy tokens using Ether to vote for your favourite candidates

How to use the app

Step 1: Install the metamask plugin and create an account on Ropsten Test Network and buy some Ether from the faucet.
Step 2: Purchase tokens below by entering the total number of tokens you would like to buy.
Step 3: Vote for candidates by entering their name and no. of tokens to vote with.
